Career Highlights

A journey of dedication, perseverance, and excellence

1996

NBA Draft

Selected 13th overall by the Charlotte Hornets, then traded to the Los Angeles Lakers.

1998

First All-Star Selection

Became the youngest NBA All-Star starter in history at 19 years old.

2000-2002

First Three-Peat

Won three consecutive NBA championships with Shaquille O'Neal and the Lakers.

2006

81-Point Game

Scored 81 points against the Toronto Raptors, the second-highest single-game total in NBA history.

2008

MVP Season

Won his only regular season MVP award and led the Lakers to the NBA Finals.

2009-2010

Back-to-Back Championships

Led the Lakers to consecutive NBA championships, winning Finals MVP both years.

2013

Achilles Injury

Suffered a career-altering Achilles tendon tear but still made two free throws before walking off the court.

2016

Final Game

Scored 60 points in his final NBA game against the Utah Jazz, ending his career in spectacular fashion.
